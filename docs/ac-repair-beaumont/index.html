What We Fix

  • Refrigerant leaks and recharge
  • Capacitor and contactor failure
  • Compressor issues and replacement
  • Thermostat malfunction and calibration
  • Frozen evaporator coil
  • Blower motor failure
  • Clogged condensate drain lines
  • Ductwork leaks and airflow problems
  • Electrical wiring and fuse issues
  • All makes and models — residential & commercial

Signs You Need AC Repair

  1. Warm air blowing even when the system is set to cool
  2. Unusual noises — grinding, squealing, banging, or rattling
  3. Short cycling (unit turns on and off too frequently)
  4. High indoor humidity despite the AC running
  5. Unexpectedly high energy bills
  6. Water leaks around the indoor unit
  7. Strange odors (musty = mold, burning = electrical)
