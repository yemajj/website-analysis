Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions Southeast Texas homeowners and business owners ask most.

Do you offer free estimates?
Yes — we provide free, no-obligation written estimates on all AC repairs, HVAC installations, and refrigeration jobs in Beaumont, Nederland, Orange, Groves, Port Arthur, and Lumberton. Request yours online or call (409) 729-4822.
Is financing available for HVAC replacement?
Yes. We offer flexible financing through FTL Finance for qualifying AC and heating replacement jobs. Ask about monthly payment options when you request your free estimate.
How fast can you respond to an emergency?
We offer 24-hour emergency service across Southeast Texas. Most same-day repair calls in Jefferson, Orange, and Hardin counties are completed within 2–4 hours of your call.
Are your technicians licensed and background-checked?
Every technician is background-checked, drug-tested, and factory-trained. Rutty HVAC & Refrigeration LLC holds Texas license TACL A26326E (AC Contractor) and TACL B26326R (Refrigeration Contractor).
What brands of AC and heating equipment do you service?
We service all major brands — Trane, Carrier, Lennox, Rheem, Goodman, American Standard, York, and more. Our technicians are factory-trained and carry parts for most common repairs in the truck.
Do you work on commercial refrigeration?
Yes. We install, repair, and maintain walk-in coolers, reach-in units, ice machines, and commercial refrigeration systems for restaurants, convenience stores, and industrial clients across Southeast Texas.
